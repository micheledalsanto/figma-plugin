## Tips for Customization

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Keep the structure (it's optimized for storytelling)
- Replace `[BRACKETED]` fields with your specifics
- Use real metrics when available
- Include concrete examples, not vague descriptions
- Show evidence (quotes, data, before/after)

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Delete section headers (they guide the reader)
- Make it too brief (employers want depth)
- Use jargon without explanation
- Claim results you can't prove
- Leave `[BRACKETS]` in final version

---

## Adaptation by Project Type

### For Client Work:
- Emphasize business impact and stakeholder management
- Include client quotes/testimonials
- Highlight constraints and how you navigated them

### For Side Projects:
- Focus on learning and experimentation
- Be honest about scope ("built in 2 weekends")
- Emphasize unique approach or technology

### For Redesigns:
- Show clear before/after comparisons
- Quantify improvements (task time, error rate)
- Explain design decisions with evidence

### For Speculative Work:
- Label it clearly as "conceptual" or "unsolicited redesign"
- Focus on process and thinking, not claiming outcomes
- Acknowledge limitations (no user testing, no dev constraints)
